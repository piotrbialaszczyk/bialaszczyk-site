About Me

Hello! 👋 I'm Piotr, a manual QA tester currently working at Samsung Electronics.

I test mobile devices in areas like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and cellular connectivity. My day-to-day work involves validating complex networking scenarios and ensuring devices meet strict quality standards.

While I enjoy testing, I’ve realized that many of the tools I use at work are internal and not widely applicable outside the company. That’s why I started this blog — to explore and document my learning as I dive into industry-standard tools and technologies like Wireshark, Charles Proxy, TestRail, Postman, and maybe even Selenium or Python scripting.

I’m not trying to be a 10X developer — I just enjoy learning, solving problems, and building things that work. I believe QA is a creative and essential part of modern software development, and I’m excited to grow further in that direction.

Outside of tech, I’m a runner currently training for my first marathon. 🏃
